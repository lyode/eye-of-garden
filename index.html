📖 A True Story

There is a story from Naples, Italy, about something called suspended coffee.

A customer would enter a small cafe and pay for two coffees.

One coffee was for themselves.

The other coffee was left behind for someone who could not afford one.

Later, a stranger might walk in and quietly ask if there was a suspended coffee available.

If there was, they received a warm coffee without paying.

No form.

No announcement.

No photograph.

Just one person helping another person they might never meet.

The coffee was small.

But the kindness travelled further than the cup.

🌱 That's why Eye of Garden sees: "Every Leaf Matters."

Some stories travel without being asked.
